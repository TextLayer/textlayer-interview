# DATABASE SCHEMA
Database: Financial business intelligence database with hierarchical account, customer, and product structures

## Business Context
- Account hierarchy (revenue, costs, margins)
- Customer geographic regions
- Product line categorization
- Time-based financial tracking

## Tables
### ACCOUNT
Purpose: Financial account hierarchy including revenue, costs, and calculated metrics
Columns:
- Key: VARCHAR (nullable) - Unique identifier/primary key
- ParentId: VARCHAR (nullable) - Reference to parent in hierarchy
- Name: VARCHAR (nullable) - Human-readable name/description
- UNARY_OPERATOR: VARCHAR (nullable) - Mathematical operator for calculations
- AccountType: VARCHAR (nullable) - Type classification for accounts
- CalculationMethod: VARCHAR (nullable) - Method used for financial calculations
- DebitCredit: VARCHAR (nullable) - Debit (0) or Credit (1) indicator
- LineItemRequired: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- NonLeafInput: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- NumericFormat: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- PreventDataEntry: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- TCMethod: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- TCFormulaMDX: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- ProceduralCalc: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
- CurrencyConversionMethod: VARCHAR (nullable) - Data field in account
Sample data:
  1. Key='40', ParentId='', Name='Gross Margin', UNARY_OPERATOR='~', AccountType='1', CalculationMethod='', DebitCredit='1', LineItemRequired='', NonLeafInput='', NumericFormat='#,###', PreventDataEntry='', TCMethod='0', TCFormulaMDX='', ProceduralCalc='', CurrencyConversionMethod=''
  2. Key='400', ParentId='40', Name='Gross Revenue', UNARY_OPERATOR='+', AccountType='1', CalculationMethod='', DebitCredit='1', LineItemRequired='', NonLeafInput='', NumericFormat='#,###', PreventDataEntry='', TCMethod='0', TCFormulaMDX='', ProceduralCalc='', CurrencyConversionMethod=''
